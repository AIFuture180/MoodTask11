Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2025

At MoodTask, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your information. By using MoodTask, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you interact with our tools (e.g., Gratitude Note, Worry Shredder), you may provide personal information such as your mood entries or notes. We store this information temporarily in your browser’s local storage to provide you with tailored recommendations.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect anonymized information about your interactions with our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on activities. This data helps us improve our services and user experience.
  • Third-Party Analytics: We use third-party analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to collect anonymized data about site usage. This helps us understand how users interact with MoodTask and improve our offerings.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering your preferences. You can manage cookie settings in your browser to opt-out of non-essential cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized mood-based recommendations and activities.
  • Improve our website and services through analytics and user feedback.
  • Communicate with you, if you opt-in to receive updates or support.

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law, with your explicit consent (e.g., if you choose to explore affiliate links like Online Therapy), or with trusted service providers who assist in operating our website (e.g., analytics providers), all of whom are bound by confidentiality agreements.

We implement reasonable security measures, such as encryption and secure data storage practices, to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of any sensitive information you provide.

Your Rights

You have the right to:
